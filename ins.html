Instructions

1. You can donate books, buy books or sell books.

2. You can choose the person you want to buy the books from or the person you want to sell the books to.

3. To contact the buyer or seller, you can dial his/her phone number which will be displayed beside their name.

4. If you want to donate, you can directly donate books without the help of the app.

5. If you want to change your role, like from buyer to seller, then you can logout and change your role.



And remember!
